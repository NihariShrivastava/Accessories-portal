================================================================================
                        ACCESSORIES PORTAL - USER MANUAL
================================================================================

Welcome to the Accessories Portal! This comprehensive system is designed to 
streamline inventory management and billing for automobile accessories across 
multiple counters.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. GETTING STARTED
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Accessing the Portal:
1. Open your web browser and navigate to the portal URL.
2. Enter your registered Email and Password.
3. Click "Login".
4. Based on your role, you will be redirected to either the Admin Dashboard or 
   the Counter Dashboard.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
2. ADMIN DASHBOARD (For Managers & Administrators)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The Admin Dashboard is the central hub for overseeing the entire operation.

A. Dashboard Overview
- Summary cards display real-time counts of Counters, Total Inventory items, 
  and active Sales Reports.
- Clicking any card will take you directly to the respective management view.

B. Counter Management
- Access via "Manage Counters".
- View a list of all active counters.
- Add New Counter: Provide a name and credentials for new staff.
- Edit/Delete: Update counter names or remove access as needed.

C. Inventory Management
- Excel Upload: 
  - Select the target Counter and the Upload Date.
  - Click "Upload Excel" and choose your .xlsx file.
  - This is the primary way to bulk-import new stock.
- Upload History:
  - Track every upload session.
  - Delete by Date: If an error occurred during a specific upload, you can 
    delete all items imported on that date.
- Global Inventory Slider:
  - A visual way to browse stock levels across different vehicle models.
  - View specific inventory per Model or per Counter.
- Editing & Deleting:
  - Click on any accessory to edit its quantity or price.
  - Use the "Trash" icon to remove an item from the system.
- Stock Transfer:
  - Move items from one counter to another.
  - Transfer Single: Move a specific quantity of one item instantly.
  - Transfer Cart: Add multiple items to a "Transfer Cart" and move them all 
    at once to a destination counter.

D. Reports & Billing
- Reports: View total revenue, bill counts, and inventory valuation.
- Bill Monitoring: View all generated bills. Click any bill to see the full 
  itemized breakdown, payment method, and balance status.
- Date Filtering: Use the date range filter to view performance for specific 
  days, weeks, or months.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
3. COUNTER DASHBOARD (For Sales Staff)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The Counter Dashboard is optimized for fast sales and local stock tracking.

A. Making a Sale (Generating a Bill)
1. Search: Use the Global Search bar to find an accessory by Name or Code.
2. Select Model: Alternatively, use the "Select Model" dropdown to see all 
   accessories available for a specific vehicle (e.g., Elevate, City).
3. Add to Cart: Click the "Add to Cart" button on an item.
4. Set Quantity: In the popup, enter the quantity being sold.
5. Review Cart: Click the "CART" button at the top right to review items, 
   adjust quantities, or remove items.
6. Checkout: 
   - Enter Customer Name and Mobile Number.
   - Select Payment Method (Cash, UPI, Bank Transfer, or Card).
   - Enter Amount Paid (if partial payment).
7. Generate: Click "Generate Bill". The system will assign a unique bill 
   number (e.g., INV-001) and update the stock levels automatically.

B. Inventory Monitoring
- Shortage: View items with stock level ≤ 5 units. Ensure you request 
  refills or transfers for these items.
- Surplus: View well-stocked items (> 5 units).

C. Previous Bills
- Access via "Previous Bills" card.
- Search and filter your counter's history by date, accessory name, or 
  payment method.
- View details of any past transaction by clicking the row.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
4. KEY FEATURES & TIPS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Unique Bill Numbering: Every bill gets a unique INV- prefix for easy tracking.
- Real-time Sync: Stock levels update instantly across both Admin and Counter 
  views whenever a bill is generated.
- Mobile Responsive: The portal is designed to work smoothly on both 
  desktops and tablets.
- Searchable Dropdowns: Use the search functionality within dropdowns to 
  quickly find vehicle models without scrolling.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
5. SUPPORT & TROUBLESHOOTING
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Login Issues: Ensure CAPS LOCK is off. If you forget your password, 
  contact the Administrator.
- Data Errors: If inventory counts seem wrong, check the "Upload History" 
  in the Admin panel to verify the last Excel import.
- Printing: After generating a bill, use the "Print" option (if available) 
  or use the browser's print function (Ctrl+P) to provide a copy to the 
  customer.
